A SAFE TO EAT AND ORGANIC FOOD PLATFORM BY THE KERALA GOVERNMENT
To give impetus to homegrown 'safe to eat' and organic food by reducing the cost of production and improving the price. Kerala govt is offering a platform to support and nurture growers of healthy local food grown using safe to eat agronomic practises.

Verified Farmers and Verified Farm

Farmers on the platform are real and verified through multi-layered security process
  • Aadhar linked profiles
  • Farms verified by local agriculture officer
  • Community based organic certifications

Closely Monitored and Community Verified Farming Information

All information about the inputs and supplies are captured.

Highly Effective Qulaity Control Mechanisms

  • Peer Reviews
  • Agriculture Officer Inspections
  • Community Guarantee

Food Traceability across the value Chain

From producer to aggregator to end customer there is a chain of information linking the food on your plate to its source.

Core Objectives of the Food Platform

A shift towards more safe and healthy food production and consumption in Kerala

The primary objective of the platform is to ensure the production and consumption of more safe and healthy organic agriculture produce in the State. The Food Platform aims to bring a mechanism to verify the food quality and traceability through a data driven approach. The platform shows us who is producing what? Where it is being produced and how it is produced from seed to market.
